# Credit-Risk-Analysis-Power-BI-Report
## Key Insights
- The portfolio comprises £106.7M in total loan exposure supported by £0.73bn in customer income, indicating strong aggregate income coverage.
- Credit exposure is primarily concentrated in customers aged 30–50, with the 40-year age group representing the single largest loan concentration.
- Mortgage holders and renters account for the majority of outstanding balances, making housing status a key credit risk indicator.
- Education and venture-related loans represent the largest loan intent categories, while debt consolidation loans present potential sensitivity under economic stress.
- Geographic and demographic diversification reduces single-segment concentration risk.
- The report structure supports management information (MI) and regulatory-style reporting through controlled segmentation and drill-down capability.
